ABC BANK POLICY GUIDELINES
--------------------------------
This document defines the standard operating banking policies of ABC Bank.

- All policies must comply with financial regulatory authorities.
- Customer protection, transparency, and responsible banking are mandatory.
- AI and automated decision systems must remain compliant and explainable.

ABC Bank Policy Governing Transactions
--------------------------------------
- Fully KYC verified customers are normally ALLOWED.
- Only block if fraud indicators exist.
- Do NOT mark high risk unless real evidence exists.
- Transactions should follow risk-based trust scoring.
- Customer history, AML screening, and fraud profile must be considered.

Special Compliance Notes:
- Fully KYC verified customers are ALLOWED unless fraud detected.
- If no fraud detected and no suspicious pattern, ALLOW transaction.
- Do NOT mark risk unless evidence exists.
